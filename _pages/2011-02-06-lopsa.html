--- layout: page title: LOPSA tags: [] status: publish type: page published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' --- I am proud to be a LOPSA member (Member #31014). LOPSA is The League of Professional System Administrators, a nonprofit corporation with members throughout the world. Their mission is to advance the practice of system administration; to support, recognize, educate, and encourage its practitioners; and to serve the public through education and outreach on system administration issues. For more information the organization, please visit their site.
