--- layout: post title: Baltimore/DC LOPSA Chapter Coming Online tags: - News status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' _wp_old_slug: '' --- A new LOPSA (League of Professional System Administrators) chapter is coming online in the Baltimore/DC area.  I'm pleased to have the opportunity to participate in the building process.  The necessary resources are being drawn together and the first meeting is tentatively scheduled for March 2, 2011 and will be held in a soon-to-be announced location in Columbia, MD.

For more information on the new chapter's happenings, visit the (under construction) website: www.crabbyadmins.org.  You can even join the mailing list at https://lists.lopsa.org/cgi-bin/mailman/listinfo/lopsa-us-baltimore-dc.

NOTE: The SSL cert that protects https://lists.lopsa.org is technically valid only for lopsa.org.  Despite any warnings your browser may throw, the SSL certificate is legitimate and can be trusted.
