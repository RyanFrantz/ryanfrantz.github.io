--- layout: post title: LOPSA tags: - News status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' _wp_old_slug: '' --- I am proud to be a LOPSA member.

LOPSA is The League of Professional System Administrators, a nonprofit corporation with members throughout the world. Their mission is to advance the practice of system administration; to support, recognize, educate, and encourage its practitioners; and to serve the public through education and outreach on system administration issues.

For more information the organization, please visit their site.
